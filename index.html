How to Start your Journey

Notice: Best experience at full screen: 1512 x 945!

  1. Enter your name.
  2. Choose your Hero.
  3. Choose difficulty level
  4. Click on "Start Game"
Game Rules

Basic game rules:

Basically, you need to type the following text sample shown on the game screen. Remember that you cannot use the key “Backspace” or “Delete” to remove the letters you typed.

Hero: each hero will have different magics.

How to defeat the monster:

You need to complete the typing before the time is up without violating the allowed percentage of incorrect words (25% of total words). The chosen hero will launch their first and final attack when you complete 45% and 100% of the text respectively.

Indicators:

  • Powder Gauge: It will be charged up every time you type a wrong word, and you will see it blinked when it is almost full. When it reaches 100%, the monster will attack, and you lose.
  • Magic Gauge: It indicates how much percentage you have completed the typing so far. You will see it blinked when it is close to the magic attack launch…so keep it up!

Difficulty level: The higher level, the more words to type and less time

Game Round: There are total 3 rounds for each difficulty level. You only can move to the next round when you win, and cannot move from one difficulty level to another.

Buttons:

  • Quit: Go to the end-game screen.
  • Play Again: Go back to the game screen and play the same level.
  • Back to Menu: Go back to the splash game screen.
